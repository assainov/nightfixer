About Night Fixer

Night Fixer is the online community that connects travellers and locals with similar interests for an authentic night out.

 

Amazing Experience

The best way to learn about the local culture is to meet and have fun with its people. Locals know the best places and people around the town, so that you can relax and enjoy the evening!

 

Easy Connection

No need to go to a club or a restaurant alone in the hope to meet like-minded people. Just request a nightout with a person you like, and introduce yourself.

 

Real Expectations

No more midnight surprises and failed parties! See the typical nightout before making a booking, and choose the one you prefer.
